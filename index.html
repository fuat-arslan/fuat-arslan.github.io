About Me

Hi, I’m Fuat — a researcher, dreamer, and builder of intelligent machines that help doctors see the invisible. I’m currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Bilkent University, where I study how to make AI systems not just smart, but trustworthy. My work lives at the intersection of mathematics, deep learning, and medical imaging — creating models that can actually make sense in a hospital, not just on a benchmark.

Research Interests

  • Diffusion and generative modeling
  • Medical imaging and physics-informed AI
  • Agentic and human-in-the-loop learning
  • Trustworthy and reliable AI systems
